EmergentSeas presents

My Planet and Me

In which a sad millennial receives telepathic communications from a distant planet.

What do you do when a telepathic planet contacts you from impossibly far away? Do you build a shrine to her in your pantry or just get super existential and jump out a window? Don't put yourself through that, that sounds ridiculous. Instead, come to this weird one man "story"telling show and find out how I dealt with it!
